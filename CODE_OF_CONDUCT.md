# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the MedEye project and its community will experience an environment free from harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being Respectful**: Treat all individuals with respect, regardless of their background, beliefs, or identity
- **Inclusive Language**: Use welcoming and inclusive language that promotes an environment where everyone feels safe
- **Active Listening**: Listen to others' perspectives and acknowledge their viewpoints
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide feedback that is kind, thoughtful, and aimed at improving the project
- **Collaboration**: Work together constructively toward common goals and celebrate shared successes
- **Professionalism**: Maintain professional conduct in all communications and interactions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments, slurs, or language based on protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harassment through photography or recording without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Advocacy or encouragement of any of the above behaviors

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:
- Project repositories and code discussions
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Community forums and chat channels
- Conferences, meetups, and events
- Social media and public communications
- Private communications when related to project business

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior from any community member will not be tolerated. Depending on the nature and severity of the incident, consequences may include:

1. **Warning**: A private communication explaining the violation and expected behavior change
2. **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary removal from community spaces for serious violations
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project for repeated violations or severe misconduct

## Addressing Grievances

If you feel you have been treated unfairly or have concerns about enforcement decisions, you may appeal to the project maintainers with a detailed explanation of your concern.
